2.2 Information we collect automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, dashboard interactions, timestamps
  • Device data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Performance data: Campaign metrics (impressions, clicks, conversions), publisher inventory metrics
  • Cookies: See our Cookie Policy for details
  • Affiliate attribution identifiers (bb_click): When an affiliate shares a Boost Boss share-link, our redirect appends a per-click UUID (bb_click) to the destination URL. We log that UUID, the affiliate it belongs to, the time of the click, the referring URL, and a coarse IP-region (not the full IP) so that if a sale follows we can attribute it to the correct affiliate. The UUID is the load-bearing identifier; the equivalent first-party cookie we also set is a fallback for buyers whose browsers strip URL parameters. Attribution data is retained for the duration of the 30-day clawback window plus the longer of (a) seven years for tax records or (b) any retention required by applicable law.
  • Voucher codes (bb_token): When a buyer purchases a Product through the Merchant of Record program, we issue a single-use voucher code in the format BB-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX and email the buyer a one-click activation link that includes the code as a URL parameter. The code is tied to the originating transaction (buyer email, product, capture timestamp), is honored only while in the issued state, and is consumed when the seller's signup endpoint redeems it. We retain the code, its lifecycle states (issued, redeemed, refunded, expired, revoked), the redemption IP, and any redeemer metadata the seller chooses to attach, for the purpose of supporting buyers, sellers, and auditors and for fraud prevention.
  • Publisher integration door (integration_door): When a publisher signs up or installs the Lumi SDK, we record which of the four doors they integrated through — browser-app, browser-extension-app, computer-app, or mobile-app. This field is used to surface door-appropriate documentation, fire the correct verification badge, scope the publisher's analytics view, and reach the right cohort for breaking-change announcements. A publisher may install more than one door; we record each one independently. The value is retained for the life of the publisher account.

5. Advertising and Tracking

Boost Boss serves ads inside publisher applications. We collect minimal data to render ads and measure impressions/clicks/conversions. We do not engage in cross‑site tracking of end users via cookies in publisher applications without publisher‑surfaced consent.

Publishers are responsible for obtaining any necessary consent from their end users for ad serving and analytics in their applications, in accordance with applicable law (GDPR, CCPA, PIPL).

5.1 Lumi for Browser App — runtime data posture

When a publisher installs the Lumi for Browser App script tag (<script async src="https://boostboss.ai/lumi/v1.js#…">), the runtime collects the following from each page view:

  • The publisher's identifier (from the script's URL hash)
  • The host and path of the page where the script is loaded (e.g. example.com/dashboard)
  • An ephemeral session UUID generated per page load (not persisted)
  • For each ad request: a short context summary derived from the page's currently-visible content — used only to match ad relevance via Benna's intent scoring

What the Lumi runtime does NOT do:

  • Read content from any tab other than the active one
  • Persist any data in cookies, localStorage, sessionStorage, or IndexedDB
  • Read or modify user-typed credentials, payment fields, or any input flagged type="password" / autocomplete="cc-*"
  • Track end-users across sites or sessions
  • Send unsolicited data to third-party domains beyond boostboss.ai

Publishers running a strict Content-Security-Policy must allowlist boostboss.ai in their script-src and connect-src directives so the runtime can load and send impression events. No other CSP changes are required.

React and Next.js publishers may prefer a component-style wrapper around the script tag. We document a 12-line wrapper at /docs/browser/react; it imposes no additional data collection beyond what the script tag itself does.

6. Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide the Services and to comply with legal obligations:

  • Account data: for the life of your account, plus up to 7 years after closure for tax and audit purposes
  • Transaction records: 7 years (tax/regulatory requirement)
  • Impression/event logs: aggregated indefinitely; raw events retained 18 months
  • Support communications: 3 years
  • Marketing consent records: until consent is withdrawn

7. Security

We implement industry‑standard technical and organizational measures to protect personal information, including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, secure development practices, and regular security reviews. No system is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If we become aware of a personal data breach affecting you, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

8. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority
To exercise these rights, email legal@boostboss.ai. We will respond within 30 days (or as required by applicable law).

8.1 California residents (CCPA)

California residents may request information about what personal information we collect, sell, or share (we do not sell), and may request deletion. We do not discriminate against users who exercise CCPA rights.

8.2 China residents (PIPL)

Residents of mainland China have rights under the Personal Information Protection Law similar to those above. To exercise PIPL rights, contact legal@boostboss.ai.

9. International transfers

10. Children
