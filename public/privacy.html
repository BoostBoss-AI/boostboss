3. How we use information

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Services
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts
  • Process payments and payouts
  • Calculate revenue share and deliver advertiser/publisher reporting
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, security, or technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax reporting, AML, sanctions screening)
  • Send transactional emails (account verification, payouts, invoices)
  • Send marketing emails (only with consent; you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support

3.1 Legal bases (GDPR / EU/UK users)

  • Contract: processing required to deliver the Services you signed up for
  • Legal obligation: tax, AML, and KYC compliance
  • Legitimate interest: fraud detection, service improvement, security
  • Consent: marketing communications, certain cookies

4. How we share information

We do not sell personal information. We share information with:

  • Service providers (sub‑processors): Supabase (database), Vercel (hosting), Google Workspace (email), PayPal (payments, both inbound and outbound), monitoring and analytics tools
  • Payment processors: to settle transactions and process refunds
  • Advertisers and publishers: aggregate impression/conversion data is shared; we do not share individual end‑user personal data without consent
  • Legal and regulatory authorities: when required by law or to protect rights, safety, or property
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Advertising and Tracking

Boost Boss serves ads inside publisher applications. We collect minimal data to render ads and measure impressions/clicks/conversions. We do not engage in cross‑site tracking of end users via cookies in publisher applications without publisher‑surfaced consent.

Publishers are responsible for obtaining any necessary consent from their end users for ad serving and analytics in their applications, in accordance with applicable law (GDPR, CCPA, PIPL).

6. Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide the Services and to comply with legal obligations:

  • Account data: for the life of your account, plus up to 7 years after closure for tax and audit purposes
  • Transaction records: 7 years (tax/regulatory requirement)
  • Impression/event logs: aggregated indefinitely; raw events retained 18 months
  • Support communications: 3 years
  • Marketing consent records: until consent is withdrawn

7. Security

We implement industry‑standard technical and organizational measures to protect personal information, including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, secure development practices, and regular security reviews. No system is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If we become aware of a personal data breach affecting you, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

8. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority
To exercise these rights, email legal@boostboss.ai. We will respond within 30 days (or as required by applicable law).

8.1 California residents (CCPA)

California residents may request information about what personal information we collect, sell, or share (we do not sell), and may request deletion. We do not discriminate against users who exercise CCPA rights.

8.2 China residents (PIPL)

Residents of mainland China have rights under the Personal Information Protection Law similar to those above. To exercise PIPL rights, contact legal@boostboss.ai.

9. International transfers

We are based in Taiwan. Our sub‑processors operate in various countries including the United States and the European Union. Where we transfer personal information internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses or recognized adequacy decisions.

10. Children

The Services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we learn we have collected information from a child without parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be announced via the Services or by email at least 14 days before they take effect.
