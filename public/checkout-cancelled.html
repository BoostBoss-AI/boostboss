Common reasons a payment doesn’t complete
?
PayPal payment cancelled or not approved
The payment was cancelled in the PayPal window, or PayPal could not approve it. A quick retry usually clears it.
?
Window closed before approval
If you closed the PayPal window or hit back before approving the payment, it never finalized.
?
Insufficient PayPal balance
If your PayPal balance is low and your backup card was declined, PayPal stops the payment.
?
Backup card declined
When the PayPal balance falls short, the linked backup card is charged — if it declines, the payment stops.
