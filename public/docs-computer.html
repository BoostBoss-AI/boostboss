Lumi for Computer App

One terminal command. 10 placements.

For downloaded AI apps built with Electron or Tauri. The install CLI patches your renderer HTML, links your publisher account, and prints a banner showing all 10 placements that auto-enabled.

Install

From your Electron app's project root:

npx @boostbossai/install-desktop YOUR_PUBLISHER_ID

Your publisher ID is shown on your dashboard. The CLI detects Electron in your package.json, finds your renderer HTML (typically public/index.html or src/renderer/index.html), and inserts a single <script> tag in <head>. No code changes required.

Restart your app. The Computer App verify badge on your dashboard flips to Connected within 60 seconds.

Tauri

v1.2 ships Electron support. Tauri integration is coming in v1.3. For now, manually add this to your renderer HTML's <head>:

<script async src="https://boostboss.ai/lumi/v1.js#YOUR_PUBLISHER_ID"></script>

And add to your tauri.conf.json CSP: script-src https://boostboss.ai; connect-src https://boostboss.ai;

Uninstall

From the same project root:

npx @boostbossai/install-desktop --uninstall

The CLI reads .lumi-install-log, reverses every patch byte-for-byte, deletes the log, and prints confirmation. Restart your app — no more impressions serve from this install.

Privacy posture

Active app window only · no persistence · no cross-session tracking.

The runtime reads only what's visible in your app's renderer at the moment of an ad request. Nothing is persisted across app launches. Native sound + video are allowed because Electron renderers run in your app process, not a browser tab.
