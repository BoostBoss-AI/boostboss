Common reasons a payment doesn’t complete
?
Card declined
Your bank may block first-time or international charges. A quick retry or a different card usually clears it.
?
Closed the window early
If you hit back or closed the Stripe step before confirming, the payment never finalized.
?
Incorrect card details
Double-check the number, expiry, and CVC — one wrong digit stops the charge.
?
Insufficient funds
Make sure the card has the full amount available, including any pre-authorization hold.
