How payouts work

How weekly PayPal payouts are calculated

Updated Jun 2026 · 4 min read

Overview

Your affiliate earnings move through three states before they reach your PayPal: pending, cleared, and paid. This article walks through exactly how a click becomes money in your account, and when you can expect each payout.

💡

Affiliates earn 70% of the commission pool on every sale they drive. Boost Boss keeps 30% to run the network and the payout rails.

How payouts are calculated

Each conversion is credited at the product's published commission rate, multiplied by the 70% affiliate pool split. For a $149 product with a 40% commission rate:

// per-conversion affiliate earning
const earning = price * commissionRate * affiliateSplit;
//            149   *   0.40         *   0.70
const earning = 41.72; // credited to your pending balance

That amount lands in your pending balance immediately, attributed via the click_id on the link that drove the sale.

Payout threshold

Balances pay out once your cleared total crosses $100. Anything below the threshold rolls over to the next weekly run automatically — you never lose it.

  • Minimum payout: $100 cleared
  • Below threshold: rolls to next week
  • No cap on the maximum payout

The 30-day clawback window

To cover refunds, every commission sits in a 30-day clawback window before it clears. If the buyer is refunded inside that window, the commission reverses. After 30 days it is locked and guaranteed.

⏳

A conversion from June 1 clears on July 1, and is included in the first Friday payout after that date.

FAQ

When exactly do payouts run?
Payout batches run every Friday at 00:00 UTC for all balances that have cleared the 30-day window and crossed the $100 threshold. Funds typically land in PayPal within 1–2 business days.
What happens if a sale is refunded?
If a buyer is refunded within the 30-day clawback window, the associated commission is reversed before it clears. After 30 days, commissions are locked and unaffected by refunds.
Can I change my payout method?
Today payouts are PayPal-only. Make sure the email on your affiliate profile matches your PayPal account exactly, or the batch will skip your balance until corrected.
