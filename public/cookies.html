1. About cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember information about you. Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, and pixels. In this policy, "cookies" refers to all such technologies.

2.1 Essential cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Services to function. You cannot opt out of them while using the platform.

2.2 Preference cookies

These cookies remember your settings (language, theme). You may disable them, though some features may not work as expected.

2.3 Analytics

We use server‑side analytics (Vercel Analytics) that do not require client‑side cookies. We do not use third‑party analytics cookies on boostboss.ai marketing pages.

3. Ad‑serving cookies

The Boost Boss ad‑serving SDK and infrastructure does not set tracking cookies in publisher applications. Impression and click measurement is performed via server‑to‑server signals and contextual matching, not cookie‑based user profiling.

4. Third‑party cookies on boostboss.ai

The boostboss.ai website may include the following third‑party services that set their own cookies:

  • Google Fonts — font delivery (privacy‑preserving CDN)
  • Vercel — hosting and edge delivery

We do not currently embed third‑party advertising or marketing pixels on boostboss.ai.

5. Managing cookies

You can control cookies via:

  • Browser settings: most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. See the help section of your browser for instructions.
  • Cookie consent banner: on first visit, you can choose which non‑essential cookies to accept.
  • Account settings: while logged in, you can update communication preferences.

Note: blocking essential cookies will prevent you from using authenticated features (signing in, dashboard access).

6. Do Not Track

We honor browser Do Not Track signals where technically feasible. Currently we do not use cross‑site tracking technologies that would conflict with DNT.

7. Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced via the Services.
