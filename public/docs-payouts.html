Payouts

Boost Boss pays publishers via PayPal on a fixed biweekly cycle. You request a payout from your dashboard, confirm with a six-digit code from your authenticator app, and the funds land in your PayPal account within minutes once a batch dispatches. This page explains exactly when your money moves and what controls the timing.

The short version.

Payouts run every other Friday. The cutoff is Tuesday 23:59 UTC — request a cashout by then and it goes out that same Friday; request after Tuesday and it goes out the next Friday (two weeks later). Minimum payout is $1 USD. Cashout requires two-factor authentication. You'll need a PayPal account to receive funds — see Section 1 below.

Why only PayPal today?

Boost Boss operates on a single payment provider for both inbound payments (advertiser deposits) and outbound payments (publisher payouts). PayPal is our current provider because it serves the largest cross-section of the global market — 200+ countries, automatic currency conversion at PayPal's rates, near-instant settlement to recipients who already have PayPal.

We know this is a limitation if PayPal isn't widely used or isn't available where you are. Additional payment and payout methods are on our roadmap and we're actively working to expand options. If PayPal doesn't fit your situation today, please email support@boostboss.ai — your input directly shapes which provider we add next.

1. One-time setup

Before you can request your first payout you need two things in place. Both live in your publisher dashboard Settings.

Enable two-factor authentication

Settings → Security → Two-factor authentication. Scan the QR code with Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password, or any TOTP-compatible app, then enter the six-digit code to confirm. Keep your authenticator device in a safe place — if you lose it, email support@boostboss.ai with your publisher ID and we'll help recover your account after identity verification.

2FA is required for every cashout request and for any change to your payout method. We don't rely on SMS — too easy to SIM-swap.

Add your PayPal email

Settings → Payout method. We just need one field: the email address tied to your PayPal account. That's the address PayPal sends your receipts to today.

  • Don't have a PayPal account? Create one free — takes about two minutes. PayPal is available in 200+ countries.
  • Already have one? Just enter the email you log into PayPal with.
  • Double-check the spelling before saving. If you mistype it, we can't pay you — funds bounce back and you need to re-enter the cashout.

Saving or changing this email requires your account password and a fresh 2FA code. We do this on purpose — anyone who can rewrite the destination address can intercept your earnings.

More methods coming.

PayPal is our day-one rail. Crypto receiving (USDC on Base or similar) and additional payment processors are on the roadmap and will appear as alternative Payout methods as they ship. If PayPal isn't available in your region or doesn't work for your situation, tell us at support@boostboss.ai.

2. Requesting a payout

Open your publisher dashboard and go to Promote. Your available balance is shown at the top. Click Cash out and a modal opens:

These dates are calculated live based on the current date and the Tuesday cutoff. You see them before you confirm — never any surprises about when the money lands.

Enter the cashout amount (defaults to your full balance, minimum $1), enter the six-digit code from your authenticator app, and click Confirm. Your balance updates immediately and the request enters the next batch.

3. How the cycle works

Picture two weeks at a time. Every other Friday is a "payout day." The Tuesday before each payout day is the "cutoff." Anything you request before the cutoff goes in that Friday's batch; anything after the cutoff waits for the next batch.

If you request cashout on…It goes out on…Status
Friday Jun 5 (right after a payout day)Friday Jun 19Next batch
Monday Jun 15Friday Jun 19Same week
Tuesday Jun 16 (before 23:59 UTC)Friday Jun 19Last-minute, included
Wednesday Jun 17 (after Tuesday cutoff)Friday Jul 3Bumped to next batch
Friday Jun 19 (payout day itself)Friday Jul 3Bumped to next batch

The cashout modal does this math for you — you'll always see the actual date your request will be processed before you confirm.

4. When the money lands in your PayPal account

Payouts are released on Friday. PayPal-to-PayPal transfers are near-instant — funds usually appear in your PayPal account within minutes of the batch dispatch. From there, moving the money to your linked bank account follows PayPal's own withdrawal schedule (typically 1–3 business days depending on country and bank).

If your PayPal balance hasn't updated within 30 minutes of the Friday batch, refresh PayPal — sometimes the notification lags. If it's still missing after an hour, email support@boostboss.ai with your payout ID and we'll trace it.

5. Or: use your balance to promote instead

You don't have to cash out. Your available balance on the Promote page can be spent buying ads for your own product on the Boost Boss exchange — same balance, no PayPal needed, no wait time, no fees on the conversion. Same publisher account becomes a small advertiser account.

This is useful if you'd rather grow than withdraw. You set a campaign budget that draws from your balance, choose targeting, and your ads enter the same auction every other advertiser participates in. If you decide later that you'd rather have the cash, the balance is still cashable any time.

6. Security

  • Every cashout requires 2FA. No exceptions. Your authenticator app code is verified server-side at request time.
  • Changing your PayPal email requires password + 2FA. Two factors because this is the highest-impact change you can make — it rewrites where your money goes.
  • You'll receive an email every time a cashout is requested, when it's batched for the upcoming payout, and when the funds are released. If you see one of these emails and you didn't trigger it, change your password immediately and email support@boostboss.ai.
  • We never ask for your authenticator code by email or chat. It's only ever entered into the dashboard cashout modal or the change-payout-method flow.

7. If a payout fails

PayPal payouts occasionally fail — usually because the recipient email isn't associated with a PayPal account, the account is restricted, or PayPal can't deliver for compliance reasons (sanctions screening, account hold, etc.). When that happens:

  1. The full amount is automatically credited back to your Boost Boss balance.
  2. You'll receive an email explaining what went wrong, with PayPal's reason where they provide one.
  3. Once you've fixed the issue — usually by updating your PayPal email in Settings, or by resolving the issue on PayPal's side — request the cashout again. It enters the next available batch using the same Tuesday-cutoff rule.

If the funds were sent but you haven't claimed them in PayPal within 30 days, PayPal automatically returns them. We'll credit your balance and you can request again.

8. Behind the scenes

Boost Boss uses the PayPal Payouts API as the rail for sending money. When we dispatch a Friday batch, PayPal moves the funds from our merchant balance to each recipient's PayPal account, then notifies us as each item processes. The relationship is between you and Boost Boss; PayPal is the plumbing.

We chose PayPal as our single provider for both inbound (advertiser deposits) and outbound (publisher payouts) because it covers the largest cross-section of the global market and supports automatic currency conversion at PayPal's rates. Additional payment and payout methods are on the roadmap — we'll add alternatives as quickly as we can.

9. Taxes

Boost Boss does not withhold taxes from payouts. You receive the gross amount and are responsible for declaring your earnings according to the tax law of your country of residence. If you need tax documentation (a summary of payouts by calendar year), email support@boostboss.ai and we'll send it.

This is general information, not tax advice. If your situation is complex, talk to a tax professional in your jurisdiction.

Email support@boostboss.ai with your publisher ID and we'll get back to you within one business day.
