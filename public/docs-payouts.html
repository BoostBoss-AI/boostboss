Payouts

Boost Boss pays publishers via international bank transfer on a fixed biweekly cycle. You request a payout from your dashboard, confirm with a six-digit code from your authenticator app, and the funds land in your bank account within a few business days. This page explains exactly when your money moves and what controls the timing.

The short version.

Payouts run every other Friday. The cutoff is Tuesday 23:59 UTC — request a cashout by then and it goes out that same Friday; request after Tuesday and it goes out the next Friday (two weeks later). Minimum payout is $1 USD. Cashout requires two-factor authentication.

1. One-time setup

Before you can request your first payout you need two things in place. Both live in your publisher dashboard Settings.

Enable two-factor authentication

Settings → Security → Two-factor authentication. Scan the QR code with Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password, or any TOTP-compatible app, then enter the six-digit code to confirm. Keep your authenticator device in a safe place — if you lose it, email support@boostboss.ai with your publisher ID and we'll help recover your account after identity verification.

2FA is required for every cashout request and for any change to your bank details. We don't rely on SMS — too easy to SIM-swap.

Add your bank details

Settings → Payout method. We collect the information Boost Boss needs to wire funds to your bank account internationally:

  • Account holder full name (must match your bank's records)
  • Account holder address
  • Beneficiary country and currency
  • Bank name and bank address
  • SWIFT / BIC code
  • IBAN, or account number + routing/branch code (depending on country)

Saving or changing these details requires your account password and a fresh 2FA code. We do this on purpose — anyone who can rewrite the destination account can intercept your earnings.

Crypto rail coming later.

For v1 we only support bank transfer. Crypto receiving (USDC on Base or similar) is on the roadmap and will appear as an alternative Payout method once it ships.

2. Requesting a payout

Open your publisher dashboard and go to Promote. Your available balance is shown at the top. Click Cash out and a modal opens:

Payout date
Friday, Jun 19
When Boost Boss releases the batch
Estimated arrival
Mon Jun 22 — Wed Jun 24
2–5 business days after release, depending on your bank

These dates are calculated live based on the current date and the Tuesday cutoff. You see them before you confirm — never any surprises about when the money lands.

Enter the cashout amount (defaults to your full balance, minimum $1), enter the six-digit code from your authenticator app, and click Confirm. Your balance updates immediately and the request enters the next batch.

3. How the cycle works

Picture two weeks at a time. Every other Friday is a "payout day." The Tuesday before each payout day is the "cutoff." Anything you request before the cutoff goes in that Friday's batch; anything after the cutoff waits for the next batch.

If you request cashout on…It goes out on…Status
Friday Jun 5 (right after a payout day)Friday Jun 19Next batch
Monday Jun 15Friday Jun 19Same week
Tuesday Jun 16 (before 23:59 UTC)Friday Jun 19Last-minute, included
Wednesday Jun 17 (after Tuesday cutoff)Friday Jul 3Bumped to next batch
Friday Jun 19 (payout day itself)Friday Jul 3Bumped to next batch

The cashout modal does this math for you — you'll always see the actual date your request will be processed before you confirm.

4. When the money lands in your account

Payouts are released on Friday. International bank transfers typically settle in 2 to 5 business days after release, depending on your country, currency, and bank's processing speed. The cashout modal shows you the estimated arrival window so you have a reasonable expectation.

If five business days have passed and the money hasn't landed, email support@boostboss.ai with your payout ID and we'll trace it.

5. Or: use your balance to promote instead

You don't have to cash out. Your available balance on the Promote page can be spent buying ads for your own product on the Boost Boss exchange — same balance, no bank transfer, no wait time, no fees on the conversion. Same publisher account becomes a small advertiser account.

This is useful if you'd rather grow than withdraw. You set a campaign budget that draws from your balance, choose targeting, and your ads enter the same auction every other advertiser participates in. If you decide later that you'd rather have the cash, the balance is still cashable any time.

6. Security

  • Every cashout requires 2FA. No exceptions. Your authenticator app code is verified server-side at request time.
  • Changing your bank details requires password + 2FA. Two factors because this is the highest-impact change you can make — it rewrites where your money goes.
  • You'll receive an email every time a cashout is requested, when it's batched for the upcoming payout, and when the funds are released. If you see one of these emails and you didn't trigger it, change your password immediately and email support@boostboss.ai.
  • We never ask for your authenticator code by email or chat. It's only ever entered into the dashboard cashout modal or the change-bank-details flow.

7. If a payout fails

Bank transfers occasionally fail — usually because the receiving bank rejected the account details (wrong SWIFT code, account closed, name mismatch). When that happens:

  1. The full amount is automatically credited back to your Boost Boss balance.
  2. You'll receive an email explaining what went wrong.
  3. Once you've fixed the bank details in Settings, request the cashout again. It enters the next available batch using the same Tuesday-cutoff rule.

8. Behind the scenes

Boost Boss uses Payoneer Mass Payouts as the rail for international bank transfers. You don't need a Payoneer account to receive payouts — we send the funds directly to your bank using the details you provided. Payoneer is plumbing; the relationship is between you and Boost Boss.

9. Taxes

Boost Boss does not withhold taxes from payouts. You receive the gross amount and are responsible for declaring your earnings according to the tax law of your country of residence. If you need tax documentation (a summary of payouts by calendar year), email support@boostboss.ai and we'll send it.

This is general information, not tax advice. If your situation is complex, talk to a tax professional in your jurisdiction.

Questions?

Email support@boostboss.ai with your publisher ID and we'll get back to you within one business day.
