Honest section

Not a fit for

A short list of who Lumi is not built for — we'd rather lose the lead than waste your week.

  • Gambling, adult, or affiliate-spam verticals — filtered out at policy review on both demand and supply sides.
  • Surveillance ad networks — we don't share publisher inventory with retargeting tags or behavioral cookie networks.
  • Apps you don't own the surface of — Custom GPTs, Discord bots inside Discord, Slack bots inside Slack. We need a surface you control end-to-end.
