4.5.2 Affiliate pool and commission split

For each Product you set an Affiliate Pool Percentage (the share of the order value that funds affiliate marketing of that Product). For every sale of that Product on which an affiliate is the attributed driver, Boost Boss splits the Affiliate Pool as follows: seventy percent (70%) to the affiliate as their commission and thirty percent (30%) to Boost Boss as platform fee. The remainder of the order — order value minus the Affiliate Pool minus applicable payment processor fees, taxes, and refunds — settles to you. For direct sales (sales not attributed to any affiliate), Boost Boss takes no commission on the order value beyond the standard payment processor pass-through fees; the entire order net of fees, taxes, and refunds settles to you. Attribution is determined by the bb_click URL parameter described in the Privacy Policy; the parameter is the load-bearing identifier and survives cookie loss.

4.5.3 Pricing models for affiliate compensation

By default, affiliates promoting your Product earn the share described above only when a sale is captured (cost-per-sale, or CPS). You may opt the Product into alternative compensation models — cost-per-click (CPC) or cost-per-action (CPA) — at your discretion through the dashboard. Election of CPC or CPA shifts compensation risk to you: you owe the affiliate share on qualifying clicks or actions regardless of whether they convert to revenue. Election can be changed forward-looking but does not retroactively re-price events that already accrued.

4.5.4 Activation-link fulfilment

When a buyer pays for your Product through the Boost Boss-hosted checkout, Boost Boss emails the buyer a one-click "Activate your account" link directed to the fulfilment URL you set on the Product, with a single-use voucher code appended as the bb_token URL parameter. You agree to validate the voucher code against Boost Boss's voucher API (/v1/vouchers/validate) before granting access, and to mark the voucher consumed via /v1/vouchers/redeem after the buyer's account is provisioned. Vouchers are single-use, time-bound, and tied to the original transaction; you may not honour a voucher whose status is not issued.

The cutoff is the immediately preceding Tuesday at 23:59 UTC; transactions captured by that cutoff are eligible for the following Friday's batch. The minimum payout amount is US $1.00. You may, in lieu of withdrawing to PayPal, convert all or part of your accrued earnings into in-platform advertising credit ("Ad Credit") at par; Ad Credit may be used to fund advertising campaigns on Boost Boss but is non-refundable for cash. Any cashout request requires confirmation via a one-time code from your registered two-factor authentication app. Buyer refunds and successful chargebacks reverse the corresponding seller settlement amount and are deducted from the next payout cycle.
