Pricing FAQ

How is CPM calculated?

CPM = cost per 1,000 impressions. An impression counts when an ad is rendered in the publisher's AI tool response or interface. We use a strict viewability standard and only charge for impressions delivered.

Do you offer CPC or CPA pricing?

Currently we offer CPM only. Performance‑based pricing (CPC, CPA) will be available in Q3 2026 as part of our Benna AI optimization layer rollout.

What's your minimum budget?

$100 minimum to start a Standard campaign. $500 minimum for Premium tier. Custom brand deals start at $10k+ monthly spend.

Are there setup fees?

No. There are no setup fees, no monthly minimums (just budget‑based), and no early termination fees. Payment processing fees are passed through at cost.

Can I get a refund on unused budget?

Yes. Unused ad credits are refundable within 30 days. See our refund policy for full terms.

How do publishers get paid?

Biweekly payouts via PayPal, currently. You set your PayPal email in Settings; we send the funds, recipients receive within minutes. PayPal handles foreign-currency conversion automatically using their rates. Additional payment and payout methods are on our roadmap — PayPal is our day-one rail because it serves the largest cross-section of the global market for both advertisers and publishers. See /docs/payouts for the full payout cycle, minimum threshold, and timing.

How is the publisher revenue share structured?

Boost Boss pays publishers an industry‑leading share of every campaign delivered through their inventory. The full revenue share, fee structure, and payout terms are set out in the Publisher Agreement, which becomes part of your account when you sign up.

What about taxes?

Advertisers pay applicable taxes (VAT, GST, sales tax) based on their jurisdiction at checkout. Publishers are responsible for their own local income tax reporting.
