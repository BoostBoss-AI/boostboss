## Below the ladder

**Early-mover pricing.** These CPMs are locked in for advertisers who
sign up before the Boost Boss network crosses 1M monthly impressions.
After that, CPMs reset to fair-market levels — but if you bought a
bundle before the reset, your remaining balance keeps the original CPM.

**Pay-as-you-go.** Available after your first bundle clears. Same CPM
tier as the last bundle you purchased.

**Free dashboard, no per-seat fee.** Add as many teammates as you want
to view campaigns and reporting at no extra cost.

**No minimums after your first bundle.** Spend $25 once, you have full
access — including pay-as-you-go and the Benna optimization engine.

## FAQ (short)

**What's the targeting?**
Real-time intent. Every impression is matched against the user's
in-tool prompt, the host app, and the MCP tool being called. No
cookies, no fingerprints, no demographic guesswork.

**Where do the ads show?**
Inside MCP-native AI tools — Claude Desktop, Cursor, Cline, Continue,
custom GPTs, and any other publisher running the Lumi SDK. You can
target specific surfaces or let Benna allocate.

**Is this a contract?**
No. Buy a bundle, run it down, decide what's next. Stop any time.

**What's a "fire"?**
One served impression. You'll see every fire in the dashboard with full
context — no rolling-up, no estimating.

**Refund policy?**
Unused balance is fully refundable within 60 days, no questions asked.
