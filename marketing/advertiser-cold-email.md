# Advertiser Cold-Outreach Email

Two templates for cold-outreach to the first cohort of advertisers.
Pick whichever fits the recipient. Customise the bracketed variables
per prospect — the more specific the intent example, the higher the
reply rate.

---

## Variables to fill per send

| Variable | What it is | Example |
|---|---|---|
| `{FirstName}` | Recipient's first name | "Sarah" |
| `{Product}` | Their product / company name | "Pinecone" |
| `{IntentExample}` | A literal user prompt that should trigger their ad | "what's the best vector DB for RAG" |
| `{ProblemTheySolve}` | Short phrase for what they solve | "vector search for AI apps" |
| `{SenderName}` | Your name | "Andy" |

Best for: companies whose buyer is a developer who lives inside Cursor,
Claude Desktop, or Cline. The intent example should be a literal
question a developer might type into their AI tool.

co-pilots, AI search, AI writing tools). The intent example should be
a problem their end user is *already* trying to solve when their tool
would be the answer.

## Notes on running the outreach

**Volume.** Send 5–10 per day, hand-customised. Cold email at scale
needs personalisation in line 2 (the `{IntentExample}` line) — if that
line is generic, the rest doesn't matter.

**Reply target.** Reply rate at 5%+ on intent-matched lists is the bar.
Below that, your `{IntentExample}` is too vague or the recipient list
is too broad.

**No call CTA.** The first email asks for a one-word reply, not a
meeting. Meetings come on email 2, after they've opted in. Cold CTAs
that ask for a call get ignored.

**Track replies.** Keep a sheet with: prospect, sent date, subject
line variant, replied (Y/N), bundle purchased (Y/N). Patterns show up
after the first 50.

**Hold publisher outreach.** Per the strategy memory, publisher emails
go out only after the first paid advertiser. Sending publisher
outreach now risks integrating publishers into thin inventory and
losing them. Let the demand side prove itself first.

## What to send back when they reply "yes"

A short follow-up with three things:

1. **Bundle structure** — paste the four-tier ladder from
   `bundle-pricing-page.md`.
2. **Dashboard preview** — one screenshot of the campaigns dashboard
   showing the intent string on a per-impression row.
3. **A booking link** — Calendly / Cal.com / whatever, for a 20-min
   intro call. *Only now does the call CTA appear.*

Reply 1 closes the loop. Reply 2 lands them in the dashboard.
