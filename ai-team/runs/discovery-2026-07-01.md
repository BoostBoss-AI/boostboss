## ★ What changed since yesterday
- **A second free-first sub-vein exists inside the beachhead: "no-signup homework solvers."** A cluster
  of tools (Edubrain, Mindko, aiHomeworkHelper.org, DeCopy, NoteGPT) offer *unlimited, no-login, no-paywall*
  answers. This is the cleanest ICP we've hit: real student traffic, explicit typed-question intent (our
  moat), a web surface the dev controls, and **zero monetization by design**. Highest-pain segment yet.
- **Caution flag on the "big-user" study apps.** TurboLearn (~5M users) and Mindgrasp (~5M students) look
  attractive but are **hard-paywalled** ($20/mo; no free plan / CC-gated trial) → already monetized, low
  pain → filtered out. Density ≠ fit; pain is the filter.
- **Secondary vein (creative/roleplay consumer AI) is real but carries brand-safety risk.** FictionLab /
  aiga_ have free unlimited surfaces devs control, but "uncensored" roleplay content is an advertiser
  brand-safety concern. Qualified but ranked below study; flagged for a brand-safe inventory review.
