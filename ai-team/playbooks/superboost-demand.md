# Playbook — SuperBoost Demand Acquisition (advertisers)

The demand mirror of Benna-Reach. Recruits **advertisers** to run direct campaigns on SuperBoost.
Same gates: discover → qualify → draft → GATE → handoff. Nothing sends until Andy approves; sends
also wait on domain warm-up.

## Who we recruit (ICP)
Products that want to reach **high-intent AI-tool users** (developers, builders, prosumers) and have
a growth budget. Strongest fits:
- **Recently funded AI startups** — capital + pressure to scale users fast (the cleanest demand signal).
- **Dev tools / AI infra / AI SaaS** whose customer *is* an AI-tool user.
- **Products already advertising** to developers/AI users elsewhere (proven budget + intent).

**Dual-sided insight:** the same AI products we pitch Lumi (monetize your users) can be pitched
SuperBoost (get more users). One prospect, two offers — tag which side(s) fit.

## Where to find them
- AI funding trackers (aifundingtracker.com, fundup.ai), **YC AI batch** — "just raised" = budget now.
- **Product Hunt** AI launches with traction (want users).
- Companies running ads on AI/developer keywords (X/Google) — budget + intent already proven.
- Our own supply pipeline — every publisher prospect is also a demand prospect.

## Qualify & prioritize
Score on: has a growth budget? audience = AI-tool users? clear product to advertise? funding/traction
recency? Tag fit + estimated budget tier. Write to `prospects` (segment = demand) + a cold `intent_event`.

## The pitch (sent as "The Boost Boss Team" — brand, AI-disclosed; never quote a fee ratio publicly)
- "Reach users **in the moment they're building** — inside the AI tools they already use. Matched by
  **intent** (what they're actually doing), not cookies."
- "**Pay for the outcome you choose** — CPM, CPC, or CPA. Benna optimizes spend straight to your goal."
- "**Fees as low as 15%.** Upload your product + creatives + objective + budget; Benna allocates across
  every placement. No surveillance, transparent reporting on every impression."

## Motion + guardrails
1. Discover → qualify → write prospect + intent to memory.
2. Draft outbound (GATED) → personalized, queue in #approvals; never blast.
3. Handoff on interest → advertiser onboarding (the Pilot upload flow).
- Same warm-up + opt-out + untrusted-input rules as Benna-Reach.

## ⚠ Strategic cadence (supply-first)
Advertiser spend follows **inventory** — the close needs publishers to point to. So: build the demand
**pipeline** now (gated drafts ready, relationships warm), but **don't push demand outreach hard until
we have publishers live** to show ("we reach N AI apps"). Recommended: supply = daily auto-run;
demand = weekly or on-demand until supply lands, then ramp.
